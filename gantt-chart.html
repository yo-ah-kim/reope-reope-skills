Milestone

Notes — Phase 0 analyses the 40-project portfolio in June. From July, Stream 1 (templates & guidelines) and Stream 2 (Pilot 10 conversions) run in parallel and deliver by end-August. September is an EL review and feedback period and the Stream 3 kick-off. In October, Reope revises Stream 1 while Stream 2 continues as EL internal teams begin converting the 30 remaining projects with Reope support. November's Validation milestone closes Streams 1 and 2 and hands the initial Stream 3 delivery over for testing. Q1 2027 iterates on Stream 3 while the EL conversion completes; Q2 2027 rolls Stream 3 CD automation across the 70 projects, ending with Stream 3 fully operational at end-Q2.
